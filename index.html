Travel

Parking

The venue does have car parking to the rear which is limited and there are two designated disabled bays. The car park is through an archway to the right of the venue when looking directly at it. As a heads up, it is a tight squeeze through that gap.

Parking in Wedmore itself can be tricky given the nature of the narrow roads and its historic buildings but generally you should be able find on street parking.

Directions

Leave the M5 at junction 22 to Burnham-on-sea/Highbridge. At the roundabout, take the third exit and then take the first right onto Harp Road. Follow the road until you get to a cross roads and take a left onto Mark Causeway.

Follow the road around to the left and then to the right onto Church St through the village of Mark and keep going into the village of Blackford. Take the second right onto Sexey’s Road and follow that all the way into Wedmore and turn left onto Cheddar road where the venue should be on your right.
